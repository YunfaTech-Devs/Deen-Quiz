How to Play !

  1. The Quiz is composed 5 knowledge areas each having 15 questions carrying varying point values
  2. You are required to answer each question by selecting from 4 options within 60 seconds
  3. If you answer a question correctly, you will proceed to the next question. A wrong answer ends the quiz
  4. Every time you answer a question, an explanation of the questions is loaded for you
  5. You have 3 life lines: 50/50, which eliminates 2 wrong answers. 1 min, which adds 60 more seconds to your time. Hint, this lifeline shows you how others have answered a question.
